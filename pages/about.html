About Us

Welcome to FOCUSFLOW, where we are dedicated to helping individuals manage their ADHD effectively and efficiently. We are a small team of four students at Lafayette College. Our mission is to provide tools, resources, and support to help our users enhance their productivity and quality of life while embracing the unique ways in which ADHD influences their day-to-day experiences.

Our team consists of four students who are passionate about mental health and are committed to offering scientifically backed strategies and personalized support. We understand the challenges that come with ADHD, and we are here to help you overcome them with practical AI focused solutions tailored to your needs.

With our user-friendly online platform, you can access a variety of resources, including AI powered PDF readers, interactive chatbot sites, calendars, timers, organizational aids, and more. Whether you are seeking strategies for time management, organizational skills, or just looking for advice and camaraderie, FOCUSFLOW is here to guide you every step of the way.

Join us today and take the first step towards a more organized and fulfilling life!
